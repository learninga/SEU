DeterLab is an open exerimental facility funded 
by the US National Science Foundation and Department 
of Homeland Security, hosted by USC/ISI and UC 
Berkeley, and based on the Emulab technology.2 It 
currently consists of more than 400 general-purpose 
computing nodes, some special hardware devices, and a 
set of tools for cybersecurity experimentation. 
DeterLab users can create and manipulate experiments 
using an intuitive, Web-based interface.
